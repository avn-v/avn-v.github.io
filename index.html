About Me

Hi! I'm Álvaro, Hydrogen Optimization Engineer for DNV. Previously, I worked as Energy Markets Modeler for the Spanish National Energy and Climate Plan.
In 2024, I received my MSc in Renewable Energies after developing my own mathematical optimization algorithm for Power-to-X hybrid plants.

Interests

While having studied an MSc in Energy Engineering, my main interests during my youth have been programming and economics. I started programming simple and silly games in C and Python during my free time at high school, while my interest in economics arose later on thanks to the conferences of Professor Juan Ramón Rallo in YouTube and those beautiful courses on Microeconomics at UC3M :)

Since then, I have felt passionate about energy, economics and programming, achieving to gather these three fields at my current role. Now, I am continuously learning about Python, Operations Research, and Energy policies and economics.
